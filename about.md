_Inevitable swaying,_

_accompany with maples._

_The time flees_

_and the avenue was fading,_

_the splendid pier in dusk and everything is vain..._

_Breaking away from shore and being unrest,_

_it's just loaf by harbor..._

----

_飘摇不可避_

_尚有枫叶相伴之_

_岁月光阴缥缈_

_道路渐行渐远_

_黄昏中码头依然伫立_

_殊不知往事已然成空…_

_只敢在港口徘徊_

_眼下，随着追忆与懊悔_

_终将自这片海湾离去……_
